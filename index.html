The Story of Aurora

In the heart of a shimmering forest, nestled amongst whispering willows and sparkling streams, lies the enchanted realm of Aurora. Here, Princess Aurora Marie Steepleton reigns with grace and kindness, her heart as pure as the morning dew. Known for her gentle spirit and love for all creatures, Aurora dedicates her days to fostering harmony and beauty within her realm. She believes in the magic of kindness, the power of dreams, and the importance of spreading joy. Her castle, a beacon of light and warmth, welcomes all who seek solace and wonder. With a wave of her hand, she can bring forth blooming flowers and summon melodies that soothe the soul.

Princess Promenade

Step into the royal court and meet the beloved princesses! From Cinderella's grace to Moana's courage, each princess brings a unique story of strength and kindness. Aurora invites you to celebrate the power of princesses.

Cinderella: Age 19. Fair skin, blue eyes, blonde hair. From a kingdom in France. Forced into servitude by her stepmother, she dreams of attending the royal ball, where she finds her prince with the help of her fairy godmother.

Snow White: Age 14. Fair skin, brown eyes, black hair. From a kingdom in Germany. Driven into the forest by her jealous stepmother, she finds refuge with seven dwarfs. Her kindness and purity are central to her story.

Aurora (Sleeping Beauty): Age 16. Fair skin, blue eyes, blonde hair. From a kingdom in France. Cursed at birth by Maleficent, she falls into a deep sleep until awakened by true love's kiss.

Ariel: Age 16. Fair skin, blue eyes, red hair. From the undersea kingdom of Atlantica. A mermaid who dreams of living on land, she makes a deal with Ursula to gain legs, leading to a battle for her voice and her heart.

Belle: Age 17. Fair skin, hazel eyes, brown hair. From a village in France. An avid reader, she trades places with her father to become the Beast's prisoner, eventually seeing the beauty within him.

Jasmine: Age 15. Olive skin, brown eyes, black hair. From Agrabah. A princess who yearns for freedom, she rebels against arranged marriages and finds love with Aladdin.

Pocahontas: Age 18. Olive skin, brown eyes, black hair. From the Powhatan tribe in Virginia. She bridges the gap between her people and English settlers, emphasizing peace and understanding.

Mulan: Age 16. Fair skin, brown eyes, black hair. From ancient China. She disguises herself as a man to take her father's place in the army, proving her bravery and saving her country.

Tiana: Age 19. Dark skin, brown eyes, black hair. From New Orleans. A determined and hardworking woman, she dreams of opening her own restaurant and learns valuable lessons about love and priorities.

Rapunzel: Age 18. Fair skin, green eyes, very long blonde hair. From a hidden tower. Kidnapped as a baby, she longs to see the floating lights and discovers her true identity.

Merida: Age 16. Fair skin, blue eyes, red hair. From the Scottish Highlands. A skilled archer, she defies tradition and fights for her own destiny, learning the importance of family.

Moana: Age 16. Tan skin, brown eyes, black hair. From Motunui. Chosen by the ocean, she embarks on a journey to save her island, proving her courage and leadership.
